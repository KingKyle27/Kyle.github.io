My Passion for Art

I enjoy sketching and painting to my heart's desire. I also love finding elements of art in the beautiful world around me that God has created. I especially am in awe of nature; it's mesmerizing to view and is just jaw-dropping. I like to get inspiration for many of my art pieces from photos of nature or portray the elements that I know make up classic landscapes.

“Art must be an expression of love or it is nothing.” – Marc Chagall
